Triangular Peg Solitaire

  • The objective of the game is to remove the pegs from the board such that only one peg remains on the board in the end.
  • Pegs can be removed by moving a peg over an adjacent peg and into an empty hole. This is called 'jumping-over' the adjacent peg. The 'jumped-over' peg is then removed from the board.
  • Pegs can 'jump-over' an adjacent peg in the horizontal or diagonal direction.
